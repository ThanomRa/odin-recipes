"Bread recipes"

Description:

Bread is a staple food made from flour and water, usually by baking.

Ingredients:

  • Flour
  • Water
  • Yeast
  • Salt
  • Sugar (optional)

    • Instructions:

    1. In a bowl, mix flour, salt, and sugar.
    2. In a separate bowl, dissolve yeast in warm water and let it sit for about 5 minutes.
    3. Combine the yeast mixture with the flour mixture and knead until a smooth dough forms.
    4. Cover the dough and let it rise in a warm place for about 1 hour or until it doubles in size.
    5. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
    6. Shape the dough into a loaf and place it in a greased loaf pan.
    7. Let it rise again for about 30 minutes.
    8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes or until the bread is golden brown and sounds hollow when tapped on the bottom.
    9. Let it cool before slicing.
