Pizza Recipe

This is a simple pizza recipe that you can try at home.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups of flour
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 tablespoon of yeast
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • Your choice of toppings (cheese, pepperoni, vegetables, etc.)

Instructions

  1. Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C).
  2. In a bowl, mix the flour, yeast, and salt.
  3. Add water and olive oil, and knead the dough until smooth.
  4. Let the dough rise for about 30 minutes.
  5. Roll out the dough on a floured surface.
  6. Add your favorite toppings.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven for 12-15 minutes.

Enjoy your homemade pizza!
