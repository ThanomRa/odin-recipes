"Lasagna recipes"

Description:

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, meat sauce, béchamel, and cheese. It's a hearty and comforting meal perfect for family gatherings or special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Béchamel sauce
  • Grated cheese (e.g., mozzarella, parmesan)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Fresh basil (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, heat olive oil and sauté chopped onion and minced garlic until fragrant.
  4. Add ground beef and cook until browned. Drain excess fat.
  5. Stir in tomato sauce, salt, and pepper. Simmer for about 10 minutes.
  6. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, béchamel sauce, and cheese. Repeat layers until all ingredients are used, finishing with cheese on top.
  7. Cover with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  8. Remove foil and bake for an additional 15 minutes or until cheese is golden and bubbly.
  9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving
