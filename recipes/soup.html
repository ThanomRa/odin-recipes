"Soup recipes"

Description:

Soup is a versatile dish that can be made with various ingredients such as vegetables, meats, and grains. It's perfect for warming up on a cold day or as a light meal.

Ingredients:

  • Vegetables (e.g., carrots, celery, potatoes)
  • Broth (chicken, beef, or vegetable)
  • Herbs (e.g., thyme, parsley)
  • Salt and pepper
  • Optional proteins (e.g., chicken, beans)
  • Olive oil

Instructions:

  1. Heat olive oil in a large pot over medium heat.
  2. Add chopped vegetables and saut√© until softened.
  3. Pour in the broth and bring to a boil.
  4. Add herbs, salt, and pepper. Reduce heat and simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  5. If using proteins, add them in the last 10 minutes of cooking.
  6. Serve hot with crusty bread.
